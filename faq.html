Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the archive. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

Is SIT Archive free?

expand_more

Yes, SIT Archive is a completely free resource. Our goal is to facilitate the sharing and accessing of academic materials without any cost barriers. No login required to browse or download papers.

How do I find papers?

expand_more

Papers are organized in a folder-based navigation system. Navigate through Institutes → Branches → Years → Semesters → Subjects. Each level shows folders you can click to drill down, with a breadcrumb trail to go back. You can also use the search bar to filter items within the current folder.

Are papers verified?

expand_more

Yes. All papers are uploaded and verified by our admin team. We check for relevance, quality, and accurate labeling before the files become publicly available in the browse section.

Can I report incorrect papers?

expand_more

Absolutely. If you find a paper that is mislabeled, low quality, or incorrect, please contact us through our support page and we'll review and correct it promptly.

Can I request a missing paper?

expand_more

Yes! If you can't find a paper you're looking for, you can reach out to us with the details (institute, branch, year, semester, subject) and we'll try to add it to the archive.

How do I switch between dark and light mode?

expand_more

Click the sun/moon icon in the navigation bar to toggle between dark and light themes. Your preference will be saved and remembered when you return to the site.

Which institute is covered?

expand_more

Currently, we have papers from SIT Hyderabad (SITHYD) with branches including Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and CSE-AIML. We're continuously adding more papers and expanding coverage across all semesters.
