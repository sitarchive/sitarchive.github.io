1 Quick Start

Get up and running with SIT Archive in just a few simple steps.

1

Go to Browse Papers

Click on Browse Papers in the navigation menu.

2

Select Your Institute

Choose your institute (e.g., SIT Hyderabad) from the folder list.

3

Navigate to Your Subject

Click through Branch ‚Üí Batch ‚Üí Semester to find your papers.

check

Download!

Click on any paper to download the PDF directly.

2 Navigation

The archive uses a folder-based navigation system that mirrors how academic resources are organized.

lightbulb

4 Downloading Papers

Downloading is simple and straightforward. No sign-ups, no waiting.

check

Direct Downloads: Click on any paper to start the download immediately.

check

Google Drive Links: Some papers open in Google Drive where you can view or download.

check

No Account Required: Access is completely free and open to everyone.

5 Submit a Paper

Help grow the archive by contributing your question papers.

1

Go to Submit Papers

Navigate to Submit Papers page.

2

Fill in Paper Details

Select institute, branch, batch, semester, and enter subject details.

3

Generate Email & Attach Paper

Click "Submit via Email" ‚Äî this opens your email client with pre-filled details. Attach the PDF before sending.

check

We'll Add It!

Our team will verify and add your paper to the archive within 24-48 hours.

warning

Important

Don't forget to attach the question paper PDF to your email before sending!

6 Submission Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to ensure your submission is accepted.

check_circle Do's

  • check Submit clear, readable scans
  • check Use PDF format only
  • check Include complete papers
  • check Provide accurate details

cancel Don'ts

  • close Submit blurry or unreadable scans
  • close Send image files (JPG, PNG)
  • close Submit partial or incomplete papers
  • close Provide incorrect metadata
